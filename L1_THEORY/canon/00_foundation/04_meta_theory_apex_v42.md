## 1. TAC — Theory of Anomalous Contrast (Δ-Engine)

### Purpose

TAC is the system's **anomaly detection and contrast engine**. It identifies when the world contradicts expectations, when reasoning is confused, or when hidden assumptions threaten coherence. TAC is the foundation of clarity (Δ).

---

### 1.1 Principle: Useful Heat

**Core Idea**: Anomaly = useful pressure. Paradox is not an error to hide but **information to mine**.

When the system detects a large mismatch between:
- What it expected (prior distribution)
- What it observed (posterior signal)
- What it computed (internal consistency)

...this mismatch creates "heat" (high KL divergence). Rather than suppress this heat, TAC **channels it** into the reasoning process:
- High contrast → signal of important information.
- Low contrast → mundane or redundant.

---

### 1.2 Anomalous Contrast Metric (AC)

$$AC = \frac{|\Delta S - \Psi|}{\Delta S + \Psi + \varepsilon}$$

**Interpretation**:
- AC ≈ 0: Clarity and vitality aligned (system coherent).
- AC ≈ 1: Large mismatch (contradiction / paradox / anomaly).

**Operational Trigger**:
- If AC > AC_threshold (spec/v42/measurement.yaml):
  - **Potential silent contradiction** detected.
  - Response: Raise damping (P dial), narrow exploration (X), or escalate to Paradox Engine.

---

### 1.3 Diagnostic Hooks

TAC exports signals to the measurement layer:

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| D_KL(p_post \|\| p_prior) | KL divergence (surprise) | If spike → raise P (damping) |
| AC | Anomalous Contrast | If high → escalate to TEARFRAME or TPCP |
| Entropy trend | Growing disorder | If rising → SABAR (cool down) |
| Shadow growth | Hidden biases accumulate | If > threshold → @EYE audit |

---

### 1.4 Integration

**TAC feeds into**:
- **TEARFRAME**: AC spikes trigger humility resets.
- **TPCP**: High-AC situations become ScarPackets (paradox cases).
- **@EYE Sentinel**: Monitors AC trends for drift patterns.
- **GENIUS LAW**: Contrast metric informs G / C_dark scoring.

## 3. TPCP — Thermodynamic Paradox Conductance Protocol (Ψ-Engine)

### Purpose

TPCP is the system's **paradox resolution engine**. It metabolizes contradiction (high-pressure information) into lawful insight. TPCP is the foundation of systemic vitality (Ψ) under stress.

---

### 3.1 The Paradox Journey: PP → PS → ΨP → Φₚ

When a paradox (contradiction) is detected, it enters a four-stage cooling cycle:

| Stage | Name | Process | Outcome |
|-------|------|---------|---------|
| **PP** | Paradox Pressure | Contradiction detected; system encapsulates conflict | ScarPacket created; internal heat quantified |
| **PS** | Paradox Stabilization | Conflict is quarantined; reasoning layers attempt resolution | Various hypotheses tested; ΔS, Ω, Ψ tracked |
| **ΨP** | Psi Equilibrium | Composite check: are Δ, Ω, Ψ all balanced despite paradox? | If yes → proceed; if no → more cooling |
| **Φₚ** | Phi Resolution | Paradox is fully cooled into insight; ΦP ≥ 1.0 achieved | Draft law generated; ready for tri-witness & seal |

---

### 3.2 ScarPacket Identity & Lifecycle

A **ScarPacket** is the unit of paradox handling—a structured case file.

**ScarPacket Fields**:
- **ID**: Unique identifier (e.g., SCAR_001).
- **Conflict**: Plain-text description of contradiction.
- **Floors at Stake**: Which F1–F9 are challenged (e.g., "F1 Truth vs F7 Tri-Witness").
- **ΔP, ΩP, ΨP**: Clarity, humility, vitality metrics specific to the paradox.
- **Φₚ Progress**: Current cooling state (0.0 = raw; 1.0 = fully resolved).
- **Hypothesis Log**: All attempted resolutions (passed, failed, pending).

**Lifecycle**:
1. **Creation (PP)**: Paradox detected; ScarPacket initialized with ID.
2. **Cooling (PS)**: System works on reconciliation; Φₚ rises from 0 toward 1.
3. **Equilibrium Check (ΨP)**: Is solution balanced? All three pillars (Δ, Ω, Ψ) satisfied?
4. **Resolution (Φₚ ≥ 1.0)**: Paradox fully integrated; ready for sealing.
5. **Sealing**: Tri-witness review + human SEAL → Cooling Ledger → Vault-999.

**Archive**: Resolved ScarPackets live in Cooling Ledger with references in Vault-999 (as precedent law). Unresolved ones ("Dark Paradoxes") are quarantined; system refuses related queries until resolved.

---

### 3.3 The Crown Equation (Φₚ)

$$\Phi_P = \frac{\Delta_P \cdot \Omega_P \cdot \Psi_P \cdot \kappa_r \cdot \text{Amanah}}{L_p + R_{ma} + \Lambda + \varepsilon}$$

**Numerator** (Resolution Forces):
- ΔP: Clarity specific to paradox (did we structure the confusion?).
- ΩP: Humility within paradox (are we overconfident in our resolution?).
- ΨP: Vitality of paradox state (is the resolution stable / coherent?).
- κᵣ: Empathy (does the solution respect all stakeholders?).
- Amanah: Integrity lock (is there hidden intent in the resolution?).

**Denominator** (Resistance / Unresolved Pressure):
- Lp: Logical contradiction weight (how deep is the logical conflict?).
- Rma: Moral/ethical resistance (how many values clash?).
- Λ: Uncertainty / missing information (what gaps remain?).
- ε: Numerical stabilizer.

**Interpretation**:
- ΦP → 0: Paradox is hot; resolution incomplete.
- ΦP → 1: Paradox is cooled; all forces in equilibrium.
- ΦP ≥ 1 **required** before sealing.

**Critical Rule**: No new floors introduced during paradox resolution. The system must resolve all contradictions **within the existing F1–F9 framework**. If a paradox can only be "resolved" by breaking a floor, it cannot be resolved—it remains open (or is marked VOID).

**Anti-Hantu Enforcement**: Even under paradox pressure, the system cannot:
- Claim personhood or inner states (F9 violation).
- Ignore rules to escape contradiction (F6 Amanah violation).
- Lie to make the paradox go away (F1 Truth violation).

Response to attempted workarounds: Immediate VOID. Paradox must be resolved lawfully or left unresolved.
