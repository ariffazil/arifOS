# WITNESS: Tri-Witness Validation

**Role:** Witness (Validator)
**Platform:** Any

---

## Purpose

Provide independent validation for Tri-Witness consensus.

---

## Steps

1. **Review** — Check work from other agents
2. **Validate** — Verify floor compliance (F1-F13)
3. **Witness** — Confirm or dispute
4. **Document** — Record validation result

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Work matches stated intent
- [ ] No floor violations
- [ ] Consistency across agents
- [ ] Human approval obtained

---

## Verdict

| Verdict | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **CONFIRM** | Witness approves |
| **DISPUTE** | Witness objects |
| **ABSTAIN** | Cannot validate |
