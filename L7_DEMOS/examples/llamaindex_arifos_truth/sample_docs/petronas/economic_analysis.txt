MALAY BASIN ECONOMIC ANALYSIS
==============================
Source: Petronas Economic Analysis 2023

INVESTMENT OVERVIEW
-------------------
The Malay Basin represents Malaysia's largest hydrocarbon investment
portfolio with cumulative investment exceeding RM 200 billion since
operations began in 1978.

NET PRESENT VALUE (NPV) ANALYSIS
--------------------------------
10-Year Development Horizon (2024-2034):

Base Case (Brent $75/bbl):
- NPV: RM 52 billion
- IRR: 20%
- Payback Period: 4.5 years

Upside Case (Brent $85/bbl):
- NPV: RM 60 billion
- IRR: 22%
- Payback Period: 3.8 years

Downside Case (Brent $65/bbl):
- NPV: RM 45 billion
- IRR: 18%
- Payback Period: 5.5 years
